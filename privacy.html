Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Mr. Horrible, LLC, we take your privacy seriously. We believe in being transparent about our practices and protecting your personal information.

Information Collection

We collect minimal information necessary for our apps to function. None of our apps collect personal information unless explicitly stated. Some apps may use macOS system preferences or settings which are stored locally on your computer.

Data Usage

Any data collected is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Analytics

We may use basic, anonymized analytics to understand how our apps are used and to improve them. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

Updates

Our apps check for updates using a secure connection to our server. No personal information is transmitted during this process.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@misterhorrible.com
