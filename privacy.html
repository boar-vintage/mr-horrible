Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Mr. Horrible, LLC, we take your privacy seriously. We believe in being transparent about our practices and protecting your personal information.

Information Collection

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. They collect only the minimum information necessary to function properly. Most of our apps operate entirely on your Mac, storing preferences locally using macOS's standard user defaults system.

Data Storage

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your Mac. We don't maintain any external servers for storing your data.

Analytics

We use basic, anonymized analytics to understand how our apps are used and to improve them. This includes:

  • Basic app usage statistics
  • Crash reports (if you opt-in)
  • macOS version information

All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users.

Updates

Our apps check for updates using a secure connection to our update server. No personal information is transmitted during this process.

Mac App Store

When purchasing through the Mac App Store, all transactions are handled by Apple. We never have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services unless explicitly stated in the app description.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@mr-horrible.com
